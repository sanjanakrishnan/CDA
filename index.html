ABOUT US

Community Development Association (CDA) is a Non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to developing local communities. Founded in 2019, CDA works to empower individuals. families. and communities through sports, education, health. gender equality and civic engagement. and advocavy for policy changes to benefit the community. The organization seeks to build strong, vibrant, and self-sustaining communities by focusing on community's specific needs and resources.Through its work, CDA seeks to empower individuals and communities to have an active role in their development. .

WHERE WE WORK

Matang Rishi Nagar Mankhurd is an informal settlement located in M-East Ward in Mumbai. Indis The M.East word one of the twenty-four administrative divisions of Mumbai and is also one of the poorest areas in the city. About 80% of the residents live in slums and there is a large percentage of migrants who have come to the cuty for work. The slum settlements are located in low-lying areas that are prone to flooding during monsoons. There is poor drainage and lack of sewers which makes it prone to waterlogging due to poor drainage. which leads to the spread of vector-borne diseases. The slum is also home to a large number of rag-pickers, who are routinely exposed to hazardous waste and pollutants. Slum dwellers also lack access to basic amenities such as clean drinking water, sanitation and health care. The region thus has high rates of malnutrtion, povery and illitracy
